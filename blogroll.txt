## Tildes / pubnixes

I'm a huge fan of the concept of a pubnix: a public access unix server. It's basically a shared *nix computer that a bunch of people have access to. Tildes are small communities on these pubnixes, inspired by [tilde.club](https://tilde.club).

- [tilde.club](https://tilde.club)
- [tilde.town](https://tilde.town)
- [rawtext.club](//rawtext.club)
